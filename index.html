about

Hey! I'm Mina, a Vancouver-based web developer specializing in front-end development. I love working on responsive design, accessibility, and working on technology that works towards economic and social good.

I was previously a student in BCIT's Technical Web Developer program, where I developed a wide range of websites from scratch, from high-end clothing stores to tourism sites.

Growing up surrounded by BC's landscape sparked an interest in sustainability, so I went to study Geography & GIS at SFU, where I built reports and maps using spatial data. (I think this is what ultimately led me to web design.)

Today, I'm happily working at Affinity Bridge, but feel free to shoot me an email at hello@minabui.com if you want to talk. Cheers!
